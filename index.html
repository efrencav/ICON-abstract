Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers you need

What is the ICON Method?

The ICON Method is Abstract Creative’s proprietary four phase transformation framework: Identity, Communication, Optimization, and Navigation. It aligns your brand’s strategy, systems, and execution for scalable growth.

How do Services work with the ICON Method?

Services is the execution program powered by the ICON Method. It offers three strategic tiers: Foundation, Pro Growth, and Market Authority. We apply the method based on your stage and goals.

What deliverables are included in Services?

Deliverables vary by tier and may include brand strategy, identity design, messaging guides, website and funnel copy, CRM setup, automation workflows, SEO, and performance dashboards.

Do I need to start at the Foundation Tier?

Yes. All clients begin with Identity. This ensures your brand’s DNA, positioning, and audience are clear before moving into conversion work or scaling strategies.

How long does a full transformation take?

  • Foundation: 4 to 6 weeks
  • Pro Growth: 6 to 10 weeks
  • Market Authority: 3 to 6 months

Who is this for?

Visionary entrepreneurs, service providers, and growth stage businesses ready to scale with strategy, clarity, and the right infrastructure.
