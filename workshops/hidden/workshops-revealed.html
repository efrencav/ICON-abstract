Workshop Formats & Pricing

Tailored sessions to meet the needs of individuals, teams, and enterprises ready to scale.

VIP 1:1 Workshop

  • Audience: Founders, CEOs
  • Format: Private 1-day intensive (virtual or in-person)
  • Pricing: Half-Day: $2,000–$3,500 / Full-Day: $4,500–$6,500
  • Includes: Custom workbook, recording, 7-day post-support

Group Workshop

  • Audience: Small teams, startups (5–10 participants)

Workshop Series Package
