Veridian Health Systems: Scaling Performance Through Data

Powered by the ICON Method™, our Market Authority tier delivers advanced strategies to drive performance, retargeting, and scalable growth.

Project Overview

Veridian Health Systems was operating with outdated digital marketing systems, leading to low engagement rates, inefficient ad spend, and limited insight into campaign performance. They needed a robust solution to optimize their growth strategy and maximize ROI across channels.

Our Solution: Market Authority™ Tier Activation

Abstract Creative deployed the full power of the ICON Method™ Market Authority™ Tier—setting up custom data dashboards, advanced A/B testing frameworks, paid media campaigns, and performance-driven retargeting strategies that aligned directly with Veridian's business goals.

Key Deliverables

  • Paid Media Strategy and Execution (Google, Meta)
  • Advanced A/B Testing Framework Implementation
  • Custom Analytics Dashboards (KPI Tracking)
  • Marketing Automation Workflow Development
  • Retargeting Campaigns for High-Intent Audiences

The Results

In just 90 days, Veridian Health Systems achieved:

  • 5.7x return on paid media investment
  • 38% increase in overall brand engagement
  • 22% improvement in cost-per-acquisition (CPA)
