Solvex Consulting: Rebuilding Brand Clarity

A full brand transformation using the ICON Method Foundation™ Tier that delivered measurable inbound growth.

Project Overview

Solvex Consulting approached Abstract Creative with a common challenge: a fragmented brand image, inconsistent messaging, and weak digital conversion rates. Despite having strong service offerings, their brand presence was not attracting or retaining ideal clients.

Our Solution: Foundation™ Tier Execution

Through the ICON Method™ Foundation™ Tier, we facilitated a full brand DNA workshop, defined core positioning, created detailed audience personas, and developed a new visual identity system. Alongside this, we redesigned their website to align messaging with client expectations and optimized for conversion.

Key Deliverables

  • Brand Purpose, Vision, Values Rebuild
  • Audience Persona Development
  • Messaging Framework
  • Visual Identity System (Logo, Colors, Typography)
  • SEO-Optimized Website Redesign

The Results

Within 6 months of relaunch, Solvex Consulting achieved:

  • 62% increase in qualified inbound leads
  • 3x improvement in lead-to-client conversion rates
  • 40% faster website engagement time
