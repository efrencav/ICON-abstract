Nexus Engineering: Scaling into New Markets

Market expansion at scale, driven by the ICON Method and executed via the Pro Growth Tier

Project Overview

Nexus Engineering faced a critical growth challenge: expanding into new geographic markets where their brand had little recognition. Despite industry credibility, their digital presence and inbound marketing channels weren't optimized for scaling beyond their home market.

Our Solution: Pro Growth™ Tier Activation

Applying Abstract Creative's ICON Method™ Pro Growth™ Tier, we implemented a strategic SEO content framework, designed and launched a conversion-optimized website, set up automated lead generation funnels, and crafted region-specific messaging to connect with new market audiences.

Key Deliverables

  • SEO Content Strategy and Keyword Optimization
  • Complete Website UX/UI Redesign
  • CRM Setup and Email Automation Funnels
  • Localized Landing Pages for Targeted Markets
  • Lead Magnet Development

The Results

Within 90 days of implementation, Nexus Engineering achieved:

  • 47% increase in website traffic
  • 28% increase in new client acquisition
  • 35% boost in lead-to-opportunity conversions
