I — Identity

Clarify what your brand stands for, who it serves, and how it shows up.

The ICON Method Stages:

  • I — Identity
  • C — Communication
  • O — Optimization
  • N — Navigation

The Power of Identity

Identity is the foundation of every great brand. This phase gives you clarity around your purpose, positioning, audience, and visual presence—so everything you build is aligned and intentional. Without a strong identity, your brand is just decoration.

What You’ll Gain

  • You’ll know exactly who you are, what you do, who you serve, and why it matters.
  • You’ll have a full brand system that guides your visuals, messaging, and decisions.
  • You’ll finally stop guessing—and start showing up with purpose.

What’s Included in Identity Phase

Deliverable Description
Brand Discovery Workshop A guided strategy session to uncover your brand’s essence and goals.
Vision, Mission, Values Define why your brand exists and what it believes in.
Audience Personas Profiles of your ideal customers: goals, pain points, mindset.
Positioning Statement Clear articulation of what sets you apart in the market.
Tone & Voice Guide Defines how your brand speaks across channels.
Visual Identity System Logo, typography, color palette, usage rules (delivered as a brand kit).

Who This Is For

This phase is essential for:

  • New businesses launching a brand
  • Growing brands lacking positioning or consistency
  • Founders rebranding for clarity, growth, or pivoting markets
