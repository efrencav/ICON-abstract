What makes Abstract Creative different?

We’re not just a marketing agency — we’re a brand transformation studio. Our proprietary ICON Method® aligns your identity, messaging, systems, and strategy so your brand doesn’t just look good — it performs.

What services do you offer?

We offer strategic brand positioning, digital marketing execution, conversion-focused website design, SEO, CRM automation, funnel design, paid media strategy, and more — all structured into our three ICON Method® service tiers.

Who do you work with?

We work with visionary entrepreneurs, established businesses, and emerging market leaders who are serious about growth and ready to scale with clarity.

How long do projects take?

It depends on your tier. Foundation projects typically take 4–6 weeks, Pro Growth 6–10 weeks, and Market Authority is ongoing (3–6 months+).

I submitted a form — what happens next?

You’ll receive a confirmation email and we’ll follow up within 1–2 business days to schedule a discovery call or provide next steps.

How do I collaborate with your team during the project?

We use ActiveCollab for project management and HubSpot for communication and onboarding. You’ll get your own client dashboard with timelines, tasks, and file sharing.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Each service tier includes a defined number of revisions to ensure everything aligns with your vision and strategy.

What if I need support after the project ends?

You can add monthly support or marketing retainers. Market Authority tier clients automatically receive ongoing strategy and performance optimization.
